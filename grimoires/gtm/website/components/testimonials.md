## Testimonial Collection Process

### Questions to Ask Customers

1. What was your biggest community management challenge before Arrakis?
2. How has conviction scoring changed how you think about your community?
3. What specific results have you seen since implementing tiered progression?
4. How was the setup process?
5. Would you recommend Arrakis to other community operators? Why?

### Permission Template

```
Hi [Name],

Thank you for being an early Arrakis customer! We'd love to feature
your community as a case study.

Would you be willing to provide:
- A short quote (1-3 sentences) about your experience
- Permission to use your name and community name
- [Optional] A photo or logo

We're happy to draft something based on our conversations if that's easier.

Best,
[Team]
```

## Visual Specifications

### Quote Card
- Background: White / Gray-50
- Border: 1px Gray-200 or shadow-sm
- Border radius: 8px
- Padding: 24px
- Quote font: Serif or italic system font
- Quote size: 18-20px
- Name: Semibold, 14px
- Role: Regular, 14px, Gray-500

### Logo Bar
- Logo height: 32-40px
- Spacing: 48px between logos
- Filter: Grayscale 100%, opacity 60%
- Hover: Full color, opacity 100%

### Stats
- Number: Bold, 36-48px
- Label: Regular, 14px, Gray-500
- Spacing: Equal columns
