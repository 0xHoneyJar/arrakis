## Executive Summary

The token-gating market is dominated by two major players (Collab.Land, Guild.xyz) with several niche competitors. No single player currently offers the combination of deep on-chain analytics, tiered progression systems, and conviction scoring that Arrakis provides.

**Competitive Position:**
- **Collab.Land:** Market leader in scale (6.5M wallets), focused on access control
- **Guild.xyz:** Strong feature set, flexible pricing, access-focused
- **Matrica:** Solana-focused, comprehensive NFT tools
- **MEE6:** General-purpose with basic Web3 features
- **Arrakis:** Unique positioning in analytics + engagement layer

---

## Competitor Profiles

### 1. Collab.Land

**Overview:**
- Founded: May 2020
- Verified Wallets: 6.5+ million
- Communities: "Tens of thousands"
- Position: Original token-gating infrastructure

**Pricing (November 2025 Update):**

| Plan | Price | Verified Members | Key Features |
|------|-------|------------------|--------------|
| Starter | Free | Limited | Basic token-gating, 24h balance checks |
| Standard | ~$49-99/mo | Up to 1,000 | Multi-wallet, 50+ chains |
| Elite | $449/mo | Up to 7,500 | AI Quiz Agent, all features |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | White-label, dedicated support |

**Strengths:**
- Market leader by scale (6.5M verified wallets)
- First-mover advantage, established trust
- 40+ chains, 30+ wallets supported
- New AI Quiz Agent feature (Elite tier)
- Strong documentation and developer resources

**Weaknesses:**
- No tiered progression system
- Limited on-chain analytics
- Pricing shifted to verified members (may frustrate some)
- Focus on access, not engagement
- No conviction/activity scoring

**Strategy:**
- Moving to usage-based pricing (verified members)
- Adding AI features for engagement
- Enterprise focus for revenue

**Threat Level:** HIGH (scale and trust)

---

### 2. Guild.xyz

**Overview:**
- Position: "Infrastructure for platformless access management"
- Focus: Requirements, Roles, Rewards framework
- Chains: 60+ EVM chains

**Pricing:**

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Core token-gating, basic analytics |
| Paid | Custom | Advanced features, priority support |

**Strengths:**
- Generous free tier
- 60+ EVM chains supported
- Clean UX/UI
- Strong integrations (Discord, Telegram, GitHub, Google Workspace)
- Flexible requirements (on-chain + off-chain)
- Quest-like features

**Weaknesses:**
- No deep on-chain analytics
- No conviction scoring
- Limited tiered progression
- Less established than Collab.Land
- Telegram support absent

**Strategy:**
- Platform play (beyond Discord)
- Requirements engine flexibility
- Developer-friendly approach

**Threat Level:** MEDIUM-HIGH (feature breadth, free tier)

---

### 3. Matrica

**Overview:**
- Focus: NFT communities, especially Solana
- Position: "All-in-one community management solution"
- Chains: 8 blockchains including Solana, Bitcoin, Ethereum

**Pricing:**

| Component | Price |
|-----------|-------|
| Premium Base | Custom |
| Role Limit Increase | $15/mo per 10 roles |
| Multi-Telegram Groups | $15/mo per group |
| Snapshot Plus | $99/mo |
| Domain Name Roles | $500 one-time (custom) |

**Strengths:**
- Strong Solana ecosystem presence
- Sales bot and webhooks
- Collection analytics
- Event management system
- Compressed NFT support (cNFTs)

**Weaknesses:**
- Primarily Solana-focused
- Complex pricing with many add-ons
- Less mature than Collab.Land/Guild.xyz
- Limited EVM support

**Strategy:**
- Solana ecosystem dominance
- NFT-specific tooling
- Add-on revenue model

**Threat Level:** LOW (different ecosystem focus)

---

### 4. MEE6

**Overview:**
- Position: General-purpose Discord bot with Web3 features
- Scale: "Most popular Discord bot"
- Web3: Added as feature extension

**Pricing:**

| Plan | Price | Web3 Features |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Free | $0 | Basic moderation, XP |
| Premium | $11.95/mo | Advanced features |
| Web3 Add-on | Included in Premium | Token-gating, wallet verification |

**Strengths:**
- Massive existing install base
- Familiar to Discord users
- Low price point
- Gamification (XP, leaderboards)

**Weaknesses:**
- Web3 is add-on, not core focus
- Limited chain support
- No advanced on-chain analytics
- No tiered progression for Web3
- General-purpose, not specialized

**Strategy:**
- Leverage existing base for Web3 upsell
- Low-cost entry point

**Threat Level:** MEDIUM (scale + low price)

---

### 5. Custom/In-House Solutions

**Profile:**
- Built by larger projects with dev resources
- Tailored to specific needs
- Examples: Bored Ape Yacht Club custom tooling

**Characteristics:**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Cost | High (dev time) |
| Flexibility | Maximum |
| Maintenance | Ongoing burden |
| Scalability | Variable |

**When Communities Choose Custom:**
- Unique requirements not met by SaaS
- Privacy/security concerns
- Budget for ongoing development
- Already have engineering team

**Arrakis Opportunity:**
- Replace custom with standardized platform
- Reduce maintenance burden
- Offer enterprise customization

**Threat Level:** LOW-MEDIUM (opportunity, not threat)

### Pricing Strategy Implications

**Observations:**
1. **Free tier is table stakes** - Both Collab.Land and Guild.xyz offer free tiers
2. **Collab.Land moving upmarket** - $449 Elite tier with AI features
3. **Guild.xyz undercuts on price** - Generous free tier
4. **MEE6 is floor** - $11.95 sets expectations for "basic" bots

**Arrakis Positioning:**
- Free tier (BasicTheme) for market entry
- Premium ($99-149) for SietchTheme + analytics
- Enterprise ($399-499) for custom + priority support
- Differentiate on *value*, not price (analytics, conviction, tiers)

## Recommendations

### Immediate Actions

1. **Don't compete on scale** - Focus on value (analytics, progression)
2. **Free tier is mandatory** - BasicTheme matches Guild.xyz
3. **Lead with conviction** - Unique differentiator no one has
4. **Coexistence messaging** - "Try without switching"
5. **Dune credibility** - Top-starred team is verifiable
