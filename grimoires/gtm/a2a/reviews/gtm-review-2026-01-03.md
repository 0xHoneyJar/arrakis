## Strengths

1. **Product-Market Fit Clarity** - Clear problem (engagement gap), clear solution (conviction scoring), clear differentiation (intelligence vs access)

2. **Grounded Claims** - product-reality.md ensures all marketing aligns with technical capability

3. **Bootstrapped Alignment** - Every strategy acknowledges resource constraints and prioritizes accordingly

4. **Consistent Story** - All 12 documents tell the same story from different angles

5. **Risk Mitigation** - Shadow mode as adoption strategy, soft launch before public, staged rollout

6. **Credibility Shortcut** - Dune Analytics team credential is verifiable and compelling

---

## Weaknesses

1. **Shadow Mode Dependency** - Key differentiator is still "in progress." Must verify completion before launch.

2. **No Customer Validation** - Strategy based on research, not customer conversations. Soft launch critical.

3. **Revenue Projections Ambitious** - $107K ARR Year 1 assumes 60 paying customers. Achievable but not conservative.

4. **Single-Person Dependency** - Founder-led everything creates bottleneck risk.

5. **Testimonial Gap** - No social proof yet. Critical to collect during soft launch.

---

## Recommendations

### Pre-Launch (Priority)

1. **Verify Shadow Mode Completion** - Confirm coexistence mode is production-ready before launch claims

2. **Collect 3+ Testimonials** - Soft launch must produce quotable testimonials before public launch

3. **Legal Review** - Terms of service, privacy policy, pricing terms before payment processing

4. **Create Partner One-Pager** - Extract from partnership-strategy for outreach

### Launch Phase

5. **Document Real Sales Conversations** - Build sales playbook from actual objections

6. **Track Attribution Rigorously** - Understand which channels drive signups

7. **Establish Support Baseline** - Measure tickets/user ratio from day 1

### Post-Launch

8. **Build Customer Success Process** - Before Enterprise customers

9. **Create Enterprise Collateral** - When first enterprise prospect emerges

10. **Review and Update** - Monthly strategy review based on actual data
